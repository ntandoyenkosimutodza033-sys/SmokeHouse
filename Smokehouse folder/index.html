The Smokehouse is one of Bulawayo's most vibrant bar and grill restaurants. Known for premium cuts of meat, expertly mixed cocktails, and an energetic social atmosphere, it's the perfect place for lunch, dinner, or a night out with friends.

Guests come for the food but stay for the music, the cocktails, and the unforgettable atmosphere.
